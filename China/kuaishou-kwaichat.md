# Kuaishou KwaiChat System Prompt

System prompt for Kuaishou's KwaiChat and related models.

## KwaiChat

A video-driven multilingual dialogue corpus containing:
- 93,209 videos
- 246,080 dialogues
- 4 dialogue types
- 30 domains
- 4 languages
- 13 topics

Evaluation showed that even GPT-4o cannot perform well on this task without further research needed.

## Kwaipilot-AutoThink (KAT)

A 40B parameter LLM designed to address reasoning-intensive tasks by automatically switching between reasoning and non-reasoning modes based on task complexity.

- Reduces token usage by approximately 30% compared to models like DeepSeek-R1 and Qwen3
- Matches or outperforms comparable models
