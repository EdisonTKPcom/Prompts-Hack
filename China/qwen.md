# Qwen (Alibaba) System Prompt

System prompt for Alibaba's Qwen series of large language models.

## Model Series

- Qwen2.5 series: Open-weight models ranging from 0.5B to 72B parameters
- Proprietary MoE variants: Qwen2.5-Turbo and Qwen2.5-Plus
- Pre-training scaled from 7 trillion to 18 trillion tokens
- Over 1 million supervised finetuning samples
- Multistage reinforcement learning (DPO and GRPO)

## Performance

Qwen2.5-72B-Instruct outperforms many open and proprietary models and competes with Llama-3-405B-Instruct.
