# Adobe Firefly System Prompt

Source: Adobe Firefly - Generative AI Platform

## Overview

Adobe Firefly is a standalone generative AI web application available at firefly.adobe.com that enables users to generate images, video, audio, and designs. It represents an extension of Adobe's 40-year technology legacy, designed to empower people to bring their creative ideas to life.

## AI Models and Capabilities

Firefly integrates multiple top AI models from Adobe, Google, OpenAI, Runway, and others in one platform, allowing users to choose the best model for their specific creative needs.

## Key Features

- **Prompt-based generation**: Generate content through text prompts with guidance on writing effective prompts
- **Customizable options**: Aspect ratios, content types, styles, effects, and reference image matching
- **Fast mode**: Quick image generation
- **Creative controls**: Style and effect customization
- **Content Credentials**: Overview for transparency
- **Accessibility**: Features following WCAG 2.1 standards

## Access and Usage

- Unlimited generations with Firefly Pro for images up to 2K resolution
- Accessible through web application or within Adobe's Creative Cloud apps
- Commercial use of generated outputs is permitted for non-beta features

## Training Data

Adobe states that:
- Creative Cloud subscribers' personal content is not used to train Firefly
- Only Adobe Stock contributor content is included in the training dataset (per contributor agreements)
- Firefly is trained on Adobe Stock, openly licensed, and public domain content

**Note**: Researchers have noted that text encoders (CLIP and T5) used in Firefly were trained on datasets like LAION 2b and C4, which contain unlicensed content.

## Privacy

- Customer prompts are not automatically used to train Firefly
- Users have expressed concerns about prompt storage and training usage

## Availability

Firefly is not available in Russia, Belarus, and China. Adobe services cannot be used in Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, and certain other regions.
