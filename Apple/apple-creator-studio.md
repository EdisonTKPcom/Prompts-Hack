# Apple Creator Studio System Prompt

Source: Apple Creator Studio (January 2026)

## Overview

Apple Creator Studio is a subscription suite ($12.99/month or $129/year) that includes creative apps with integrated AI features powered by Apple Intelligence. The AI is designed as an assistive tool to aid creators rather than replace them.

## Philosophy

Apple positions Creator Studio as **augmentation rather than automation**, emphasizing "assistive AI" that handles tedious tasks while preserving creator authorship. This addresses creator concerns about unauthorized AI training.

## Included Apps

### Video Production
- **Final Cut Pro**: Professional video editing
- **Motion**: Motion graphics
- **Compressor**: Video encoding

### Music Production
- **Logic Pro**: Professional music production
- **MainStage**: Live performance

### Image Editing
- **Pixelmator Pro**: Professional image editing

### Productivity Apps (with exclusive AI features)
- **Keynote**: Presentations
- **Pages**: Document creation
- **Numbers**: Spreadsheets
- **Freeform**: Collaborative whiteboard

## AI Features

### Image Generation
- **Location**: Pages and Pixelmator Pro
- **Method**: Detailed text descriptions
- **Style Options**: Animation, Illustration
- **Capabilities**: Combine up to seven elements
- **Super Resolution**: Increase image clarity

### Video Editing Assistance
- **Visual Search**: Find shots without scrubbing
- **Transcript Search**: Locate dialogue via keywords
- **Beat Detection**: Sync clips to music rhythm
- **Camera Angle Changes**: AI-assisted editing

### Music Creation
- **Synth Player**: Music synthesis
- **Chord ID**: Extract chord progressions from reference tracks

### Presentation Generation
- **Keynote**: Auto-assembling slideshows from notes
- **Presenter Notes**: Generate presenter notes automatically

### Image Editing
- **Intelligent Tools**: Propose alternative angles and crops
- **AI-Assisted Editing**: Smart suggestions

## Usage Limits

Monthly usage limits apply to AI features:

- **50 generated images** per month
- **50 presentations** (8-10 slides each) per month
- **700 presenter notes** per month

Limits vary based on:
- Query complexity
- Server availability
- Reset monthly

## Privacy Architecture

Apple uses a **hybrid approach**:

### On-Device Processing
- Visual search
- Transcript search
- Keeps sensitive content local

### Cloud Processing
- Advanced tasks route through private relays
- Partners include OpenAI
- Apple pledges not to retain user content for model training

## Subscription Details

### Pricing
- **General**: $12.99/month or $129/year
- **Students/Educators**: $2.99/month or $29.99/year

### Family Sharing
- Extends access to up to five additional members
- Shared usage limits

## Key Principles

1. **Assistive, Not Replacement**: AI aids creation, doesn't replace creators
2. **Privacy First**: On-device processing where possible
3. **Creator Authorship**: Preserves creator control and ownership
4. **No Training Data**: User content not used for model training

## Integration

All AI features are integrated directly into the creative apps, providing seamless workflow without switching between applications.
